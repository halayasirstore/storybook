About Storio

Storio is developed using the Flutter framework and uses AI tools to generate educational stories and lessons to enhance your English skills. The app is designed to be simple, fun, and user-friendly, as shown in our interactive learning interfaces above.

Information We Collect

We collect limited data to ensure the app works properly and provides a better learning experience.

a. Automatically Collected Information

When you use Storio, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version, device ID)
  • App usage statistics and crash logs
  • IP address and general location (for analytics and ad services)
  • Advertising identifiers (for personalized ads via Google AdMob)

b. User-Provided Information

If you create an account or use certain features, we may collect:

  • Your name or nickname
  • Email address (optional, if used for login or support)
  • Learning progress or preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Personalize your English learning experience
  • Improve app performance and content quality
  • Provide technical support
  • Show relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • Analyze user behavior to enhance features

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for profit.

Advertising and Analytics

We use Google AdMob to display ads in the app. AdMob may use cookies and identifiers to personalize ads.

For more information, please visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You can manage ad personalization in your device settings.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection against all potential threats.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years old. If you believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Third-Party Services

Storio may use third-party services (like AdMob and analytics tools) that have their own privacy policies. Please review their terms before using those features.
